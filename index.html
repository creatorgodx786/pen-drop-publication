Our Outstanding Distributed Collections

That Stage of Life

Echoes of Friendship & Connection

Compiled by Anagha Krishna V P. This anthology gathers the fragmented memories of connection, the unspoken vows of loyalty, and the warm, steady presence of those we call friends.

Meaning of Life

Heartfelt Voices & Reflections

Compiled by Kanchan Butola and R. Pavithra. Written in both English and Hindi, this book captures the universality of human emotions while embracing deep cultural richness.

The Story of Rebels

Bilingual Anthology

Project-managed by Prachi Malhotra, Anjali Verma, and Rakhi Jha. A powerful collection of stories and poems crafted by passionate writers who dared to express the unheard.

Why Am I Still Living?

Profound Inquiry & Verse

By Supriya C.S.K. A poignant inquiry that transcends mere curiosity about life; it evokes the deepest sentiments regarding existence, purpose, and personal significance.

In Search Of I

Self, Soul & Silence

By Khushi Singh Rajput. A beautiful bilingual tapestry of poetry and musings speaking straight from the heart, searching for identity, truth, and inner stillness.

Crimson Silence

Heartfelt Verses

Compiled by Prachi Malhotra. A striking collection where each line bleeds emotion—exploring love, heartbreak, hope, and the echo of unspoken words.
